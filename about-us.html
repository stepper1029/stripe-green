About Us

Meet the coders and environmental activists behind Stripe Green! We are committing ourselves to educate others on the impacts of climate change and explore ways to have a more sustainable lifestyle.

Molly

Olivia

Claire

Molly is a rising high school senior from Chappaqua, NY. She has a great interest in biology and environmental science and policy, which drives her support of the environment. Molly is dedicated to living a more sustainable life. To do this, she has reduced the amount of single-use plastic she uses by switching to reusable straws, opting for reusable, silicone food storage bags and using reusable water bottles. She also cut out all meat from her diet and looking for plant-based meal alternatives. Molly also makes an effort to support eco-conscious brands, like Allbirds for sneakers.

Olivia is a rising high school junior from Nyack, NY. She is passionate about environmental activism and wants to use computer science to spread information as well as express herself and her interests. Olivia participates in global and local environmental movements and is always learning how to live more sustainably. She tries to live sustainably by finding meat alternatives, consuming little plastic waste, eating locally sourced foods, and buying second-hand clothes.

Claire is a rising high school junior from Nyack, NY. She began programming this summer and wanted to collaborate with her peers to create a platform for their environmental activism. Claire is passionate about environmental justice and makes sure to apply the movement to her everyday life. She participates in activist groups like Extinction Rebellion Youth and takes small steps to make her life more sustainable. These include shopping from ethical companies, reducing her plastic consumption, and not eating red meat.
