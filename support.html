Allbirds

Allbirds is a show brand that is committed to have sustainable manufacturing practices. Allbirds works hard to reduce their carbon footprint by using natural and recycled materials for their products and offsetting their carbon emissions by taxing themselves and investing that money into programs that will reduce their carbon footprint. Allbirds currently perates as a carbon neutral business and hopes to become "carbon negative" in the future.

  • Coca-Cola
  • PepsiCo
  • Nestlé
  • Fast Fashion: Romwe, Shein, Forever 21

Fast Fashion

The fashion industry is the second largest polluter in the world, especially fast fashion. The Intergovernmental Panel on Climate Change remarks that the fashion industry produces 10% of global carbon dioxide emissions every year, and is estimated to use around 1.5 trillion liters of water annually. Additionally, fast-fashion factories release harmful chemicals into the environment and contribute immensely to landfill waste. Try supporting more eco-conscious brands like Reformation, or opt to shop at secondhand clothing stores.

Extinction Rebellion

Extinction Rebellion is an international, non-partisan movement based in the UK whose mission is to push world governments to take the climate crisis seriously through civil disobedience. Extinction Rebellion has influenced the passage of legislation. Check out their website for ways to get involved.

Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez

Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez (AOC) is the Representative for New York's 14th congressional district and a member of the Democratic party. Along with politician Ed Markey, AOC introduced the Green New Deal, a framework for how the US can have a sustainable, functional and thriving economy. The plan involves creating millions of high wage jobs in new green industries, converting to renuable energy, restoring natural ecosystems, working with farmers to reduce pollution and greenhouse gas emission from agriculture and much more.

Avoid these Politicians:

  • Donald Trump
  • Mike Pence
  • Lee Zeldin

Donald Trump

Donald Trump, the 45th President of the US, has dismissed the severity of the climate crisis countless time. During his time as president, Trump has repealed critical pieces of legislation such as the Clean Power Plan and withdrew from the Paris Agreement on climate chenge. He has been known to ignore and dismiss the science behind climate change. If the world wants to get the environmental crisis under control, a more progressive US president is necessary.
