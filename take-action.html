Take Action!

Join the Movement!

Even the smallest actions are a step in the right direction - everything counts! If we band together, we can create real change. Sustainability Checklist

Here are three steps you can take to work towards living more sustainably. Check off the boxes after you have completed the task!

  • Educate yourself on environmental issues.
  • Take at least one action from our to be more sustainable. Get ideas from our sustainability tab.
  • Contact your local legistlative representatives and let them know you support climate justice. For more info, click here.
  • Share what you know and encourage others to implement more sustainable practices into their lives too!
